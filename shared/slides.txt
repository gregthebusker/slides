      <h2>Fullscreen-only &lt;video&gt;</h2>
    </hgroup>
    <article class="build">
      <div>
        <h3>Video element can be stuck anywhere on the page</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>1x1 pixels, or offscreen</li>
          <li>but: has to be in the DOM, can't be visibility:hidden or display:none</li>
          <li>just call play() to go fullscreen</li>
          <li>iPhone: zoom-in animation from &lt;video&gt;'s position</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h3>On webkit, good interaction with your webapp</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Video events still fire: timeupdate, ended etc.</li>
          <li>Free to change web page in the background</li>

    <hgroup>
      <h2>Appcache gotchas</h2>
    </hgroup>
    <article class="build">
      <ul>
        <li>GET parameters on cached resources bypass the cache</li>
        <li>Strict implementations (eg. iOS5) refuse to download files not in the manifest</li>
        <li class="li2">since YouTube streams from many domains, we just put <pre>NETWORK: *</pre></li>
      </ul>
      <ul>
        <li>Can get wrong in a scary way</li>
        <li class="li2">Users stuck on old code</li>
        <li class="li2">Create multiple copies of the entire site in the cache</li>
      </ul>
      <ul>
        <li>Easy to break master URL</li>
        <li class="li2">some redirects to an appcached site: 303 or history.replaceState</li>
        <li class="li2">history.pushState before background download completes</li>
        <li>Some implementations are buggy</li>
      </ul>
